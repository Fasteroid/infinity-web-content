Guidelines & Server Rules
Basically, just don't piss on each other or the server, that's about it.
  • Remember to share server CPU!
    • Keep an eye on the global E2 usage meter posted at spawn.
    You can move it for yourself by left-clicking anywhere with the E2 Usage Meter Mover Tool (found under GUI Panels)
    It should ideally stay below 100%, but can go as high as 130% without significant server lag.

    You can use the below loop to limit any contained code to MaxCPU μs of CPU time:
    while( cpuUsage()*10^6 < MaxCPU ){ doSomething() }
  • Don't spam.
    • If you spawn enough of it to cause a disturbance to the server, you're spamming.
    This includes non-entities such as bullets, trails, PAC3 anything, sounds, and even light cast by lamps.
    Dupes that freeze the server when spawning also fall under this category.
  • Don't be a dick.
    • Don't bother other players on purpose—please use common sense!
    If a player asks you to stop treating them or their stuff a certain way, you should.
    No personal attacks—things like racism and sexism will probably get you banned!
  • You may prop minge, BUT...
    • Whatever you do, make sure you don't lag or crash the server in the process.
    Be mindful of the earthquakes you cause, as they may bother those around you.
    Make sure you're ready to mash Z at a moment's notice if things go south.
  • Avoid spawnkill.
    • Mass spawnkill is a no-no; don't make turret wheels or instakill death props in spawn.
    Stationary defenses and NPCs should not be placed in spawn.
    Try to give players at least three seconds before you damage them.
  • Regarding Clientside Lua...
    • Clientside cheats are ALLOWED and you will NOT BE BANNED for using them.
    Anything goes so long as you don't abuse the net library or exploits to crash the server or clients.
    In the event you're targeted by a cheater, you can either enable god or try to counter their cheat.
Otherwise, just do your best to enjoy yourself!
