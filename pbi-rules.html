Guidelines & Server Rules

At least glance over these, would ya?

  • Don't ghost!
    To keep things simple, just avoid mentioning any locations on the map period.
    Hunters, use F4 to spot fleeing stragglers instead of calling it out in voice!  It's much more effective!
    Props, please do not livestream anything with a delay of less than 5 minutes—it puts the entire team at risk!
  • Props, please hide fairly!
    At least one vertex of your model should be showing.  Other than that, you may hide inside any mesh you choose.
    You must be somehow killable by default weapons.  If the Hunters can't figure this out however, that's their problem.
  • Hunters, please don't cheat!
    You already have a lot of cool tech available to you in the pointshop.
    There is no reason you should need to resort to clientside lua to find them.
  • Don't be a dick.
    No personal attacks—things like racism and sexism will get you banned if they offend someone.

Good luck, and may the best hider win!
