Guidelines & Server Rules

At least glance over these, would ya?

  • Don't ghost!
    To keep things simple, avoid mentioning any locations on the map period.  DEFINITELY don't give away people's hiding spots.
    Hunters, spot fleeing stragglers with 'Q' (or bind ph_spot) instead of calling them out in voice.  It's worth it, trust me.
    Please don't livestream anything with a delay of less than 5 minutes here—it puts the props at a disadvantage.
  • Props, please hide fairly!
    Three or more verticies of your model must be showing.  This is really only applicable if you're hiding inside a buggy mesh.
    You must be somehow killable by default weapons.  Don't glitch inside walls if you can help it!
  • Hunters, please don't cheat!
    You already have a lot of cool tech available to you in the pointshop (F3).
    There is no reason you should need to resort to clientside lua to find them.
    If you really want to test your cheats, you might like our sister sandbox server, E2 Beyond Infinity.
  • Don't be toxic.
    Petty insults and trash talk are fine in moderation, just don't make anyone leave.
    Be a good sport—please don't egg people on if you're already dominating them.
    No personal attacks—things like racism and sexism will get you banned if they offend someone.
  • Have fun!
    Even bad spots can be good—especially if they get a laugh out of everyone.
    While encouraged, hunters don't have to kill the props.  Other various tomfoolery can be just as fun.
    If you find yourself suffering from decision fatigue, stop playing and take a short break.  They work wonders on the mind.

Otherwise, good luck, and may the best hider win!

  — Fasteroid :)
